A short rundown of how I prepped for the CCNA over about twelve weeks.

## What worked

- **Official Cert Guide** — dense but comprehensive. Pacing it 1–2 chapters per
  week beat trying to cram.
- **Boson ExSim** — the closest thing to the real exam. Use it as a diagnostic,
  not a study tool.
- **Packet Tracer labs** — the muscle memory for VLANs, OSPF, and ACLs only
  comes from hands-on time. I rebuilt every topology from the book at least
  once.

## What I'd skip

Long video courses on 1.5x speed. Useful for getting unstuck on a single topic;
not useful as a primary path through the material.
